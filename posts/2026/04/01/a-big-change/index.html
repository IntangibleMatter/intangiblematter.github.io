A Big Change

Published on 2026-04-01  🞂 1 min read
Tags: general personal update


Hey! So it’s been a while since I’ve posted an update. I keep meaning to but I just can’t figure out what to write. That said, I do have a major announcement today:

I am now tangible.

That’s right. I’m no longer something which cannot be interacted with. I realized that I didn’t like being intangible, and so I’m changing my ways. I know that this will be a major adjustment for some people, but I promise I’m still the same person you know and love.

I’m now going to be known as TangibleMatter. This feels like a step towards being much more true to myself, and I look forward to living life with this evolution of the name I’ve been using for almost a decade.

Thanks for supporting me in this <3
